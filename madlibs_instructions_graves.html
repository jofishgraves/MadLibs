MadLibs Instructions

How to play

  • You'll be given a list of different prompts. This can include:
    • A noun
    • A person
    • A verb
    • An adjective
    • etc.
  • The story will show you where the resulting prompts will go before you submit.
  • Your task is to fill in the blanks and it'll generate a short story with the prompts.
  • One you've finished filling your prompts, click the "Submit" button and the story will generate.
  • Your responses will be in a bracket.
    • [example]
  • There are multiple stories and you can select which story to use before submtting prompts.
